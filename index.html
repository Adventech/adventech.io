Adventech

 

We help Seventh-Day Adventist Churches and Ministries with tech. For free.

Mission

Our mission is to glorify the Ancient of Days by helping official Adventist ministries and churches in technology. By empowering passionate and technologically advanced believers we want to up the bar of existing resources that support Adventist initiatives in this digitally connected world.

Vision

We believe that God blessed people and provided His wisdom and guidance in technology advancements. We also believe that we need to maximize the efforts of use of these technologies for the Glory of Almighty.
